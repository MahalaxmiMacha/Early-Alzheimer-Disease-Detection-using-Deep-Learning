Alzheimer Disease Types

Alzheimer’s Disease (AD)

A progressive neurological condition causing memory loss, confusion, and personality changes. Brain imaging reveals shrinkage, especially in the hippocampus.

Cognitively Normal (CN)

Individuals with no memory problems or impairment. Used as a control group in research for comparison with affected groups.

Early Mild Cognitive Impairment (EMCI)

Mild memory or concentration issues that don’t yet affect daily life. Early diagnosis can significantly help progression control.
